Overview

Our task is to predict the PISA test scores for 15-year-old students based on their family and school information. Our inputs include basic personal information, family educational/career background, educational/school resources, and learning experiences for each student taking the PISA exam. Our output is simply a predictive score for the critical reading section of the test for each candidate.
